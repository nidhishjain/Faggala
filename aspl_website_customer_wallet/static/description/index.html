Odoo 13 Customer Wallet (Community)

This module allows customer to use their wallet while making the order.

Note: This module is tested and working in Odoo community edition but not tested in Enterprise version. And we are supporting Ubuntu OS and not Windows OS.

How to use

  • In Website -> Configuration -> Setting, You can Enable the Option Customer Wallet.


  • On the Website side you can see the Wallet menu click on that menu see the Wallet Screen.


  • When you click on the Wallet you can redirect on the Set Pic screen where first time you can set the pin then you need to that pin from where you can login to wallet screen.


  • After that you can see the Wallet screen on the screen you can see three button, when you click on the Passbook button you can see the your transaction History, when you click on the Add Money on that you can add the money in your wallet and On Change Pin, you can change your pin.


  • When you click on the Add Mondey then you will see Add money to Wallet field, you can enter the amount on that field and after that you need to pay that amount and at the end you can see the one ticket of the your wallet transaction.


  • One mail should go to the customer that you have add the amount in the wallet.


  • When you Shop the Product then you can pay the amount from your wallet, and one mail should go to the customer that you have used the amount from the wallet.


  • After that you can also check the wallet transcations on the wallet screen.


  • If you don't have a sufficient wallet amount then you can use the your current wallet amount while make the order and you need to pay only remaining amount after the wallet amount is decreased from the Total order amount.


  • When customer will click on the time slot then service will be booked for that particular time and customer will redirect on the Payment page, after that select the payment acquirer and make the payment and then you will be redirect on the confirmation page and also mail should go to the customer about the wallet transaction.


  • In Invoicing -> Customer -> Wallet, you can see the all the wallet transactions of all the customers.
